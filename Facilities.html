Facilities we provide

Hostel

College provides hostel facilities. There is a common dinning room, accommodates three students. A full time warden to look after the internal administration & day to day discipline of the hostel.

Cafeteria

A well equipped canteen provides lunch.

Transportation

The college provides transportation facilities for taking students to college & to the attached hospitals as well as urban & rural training centers escorted by our teaching staffs.

Labs

Nursing practice laboratory consists of 12 beds, modern mannequins & other articles required for practicing nursing procedures. Nutrition laboratory having facilities for imparting basic knowledge about food & for practice in cooking poor for the sick. Community nursing & obstetrics laboratory having all articles required for practicing nursing procedure in a community set up. Museum consists of models, charts, bones.

Library

Comfortable seating arrangements for students & staffs with proper lighting & ventilation. There are sufficient number of cupboards, racks for books. There are enough number of books for the further reference & for the benefit of students.

Multipurpose Hall

Is a spacious enough to accommodate all the students at a time. It is utilised for hosting functions of the college, educational conferences, workshops, examinations etc., The arrangements for the use of all kinds of basic & advanced audio visual aids are available.
